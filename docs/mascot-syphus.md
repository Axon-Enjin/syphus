# Mascot Brief: Sisu the Ledger Crab

**Project:** Syphus
**Status:** Draft
**Last updated:** 2026-07-13
**Design reference:** Crustle (Pokémon) — *reference only, see Legal distance*

---

## 1. Name

**Sisu** — Finnish for "stoic grit and perseverance"; half-rhymes with Sisyphus.

Alternatives considered: **Crest** (cresting the hill; a coin has a crest), **Rollo**.
Do **not** shorten the brand name to "Syph" for the mascot.

---

## 2. The Story

Every freelancer knows the boulder: chase the client, send the invoice, wait, follow up, wait again. Sisu is a small crab who spent forever pushing a boulder up that hill — until the boulder turned into a coin and started rolling *itself* down to her. Now she rides it.

**The pitch in one image: Syphus turns the boulder into the coin.**

---

## 3. Design

Base reference is Crustle — a small, determined creature hauling a burden bigger than itself. Reference, not template.

| Element | Spec |
|---|---|
| Body | Small, low, wide-stanced crab — big head, tiny legs, oversized front claws |
| Shell | Trust green `#0D6E4F` (DSD `--color-accent`) |
| Belly | Off-white `#FAFAF8` (DSD `--color-bg`) |
| Outlines | Thick `#1A1A18` (DSD `--color-text`), flat/duotone illustration style |
| The burden | Giant round **coin** on her back, bigger than she is — amber `#B45309` rim (DSD `--color-warning`), off-white face, embossed with the Syphus mark |
| Face | Two big determined half-lidded eyes, small flat mouth — "tired but absolutely not stopping" |

### Legal distance from Crustle

Pokémon designs are Nintendo/Game Freak IP. Keep the *trope* (small creature, huge burden); drop every literal feature:

- **4 legs** instead of 6
- **Round coin** instead of rectangular rock slab (the biggest silhouette differentiator)
- **Green/amber** palette instead of orange/brown
- **Round eyes** instead of Crustle's angular ones
- **No rocky texture** anywhere

The final mascot must be an original character — original species, DSD palette colors, no direct trace of any Pokémon.

---

## 4. Personality

Quiet grit. Never panics, never celebrates loudly — the biggest emotion she shows is one raised claw at the summit. She doesn't talk in UI copy; she just *appears in the right state*, which keeps the brand from getting cutesy in a product that moves money.

---

## 5. Pose per Product State

| Product state | Sisu pose |
|---|---|
| Payment pending | Pushing the coin uphill, leaning hard (amber tint) |
| Processing / on-chain confirm | Coin cresting the top of the hill |
| Payment confirmed | Sitting on top of the coin at the summit, one claw up (success green `#047857`) |
| Empty dashboard | Napping against the coin — "nothing to push right now" |
| Error / failed tx | Coin rolled back a step; Sisu dusting off, already re-gripping |
| Onboarding | Strapping the coin onto her back |
| 404 | Alone at the bottom of the hill, looking around |

---

## 6. Illustrator / Image-Model Prompt

> A small cartoon hermit-crab-like mascot character, flat vector style with bold dark outlines, carrying a giant round gold-amber coin on its back that is twice its size. Deep green (#0D6E4F) rounded shell body, cream belly, four stubby legs, two oversized claws, big determined half-lidded eyes. Warm off-white background (#FAFAF8). Friendly fintech brand mascot, sticker-style, no text. Original character — not a Pokémon.

## 7. Alternate Pokémon References (not pursued)

Kept for the record from the initial ideation:

- **Dwebble** — Crustle's pre-evolution; small, scrappy, cute
- **Rolycoly / Carkol** — rolling rock with a wheel-eye; good motion language for a loading spinner
- **Phanpy / Donphan** — curls up and rolls like a wheel
- **Gimmighoul** — tiny creature guarding a chest of coins
- **Gholdengo** — made of 1,000 gold coins; too literal/greedy for a trust-centric payment brand
- **Meowth** — the classic coin-on-forehead reference
